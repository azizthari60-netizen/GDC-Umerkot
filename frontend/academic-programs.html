Academic Programs

Explore the diverse academic programs offered by our college, designed to empower students with knowledge and skills for a successful future.

Intermediate Programs

Pre Medical

A 2-year intermediate program that prepares students for medical and health science fields, covering subjects like Biology, Chemistry, and Physics.

Pre Engineering

A 2-year intermediate program that equips students with foundational knowledge in Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry for engineering disciplines.

Commerce

A 2-year intermediate program focused on business studies, accounting, economics, and commerce-related subjects to prepare students for careers in business and finance.

Arts

A 2-year intermediate program that offers a broad range of subjects in humanities, social sciences, and languages to foster critical thinking and creativity.

BS Programs

BS Chemistry

A comprehensive 4-year undergraduate program that provides in-depth knowledge of chemical principles, laboratory techniques, and research methodologies.

BS Computer Science

An intensive 4-year undergraduate program that covers fundamental concepts of computer science, programming, and software engineering.
